Under the Hood

How the AI makes a decision

A transparent, four-stage pipeline from raw application to explainable outcome.

01

Data Ingestion

Applicant details are validated, cleaned and engineered into model-ready features (e.g. debt-to-income ratio).

02

Model Scoring

The best-performing model — selected automatically by ROC-AUC — scores the application in milliseconds.

03

Explainability Layer

Feature contributions are computed per-applicant to surface the top factors driving the decision.

04

Risk Classification

Approval probability is mapped to Low / Medium / High risk with tailored next-step recommendations.

Benchmarks

Four models. One winner, chosen by data.

Every model is trained and evaluated on the same holdout set; the highest ROC-AUC is deployed to production.
